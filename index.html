IHow We Collect & Use Your Information
Account & Profile

We collect your email or phone, username, and password to create your account. Optional at registration: gender, interests, date of birth. Via Google Sign-In, we receive your name, email, and profile photo. Your profile photo, bio, and location are publicly displayed and editable at any time. Verified Sellers must provide BVN or government-issued ID — used solely for verification, never publicly displayed.

Content & Social Interactions

We collect your posts, likes, reposts, comments, searches, and interaction patterns to power content recommendations. Adjust personalisation at Me → Settings → Privacy → Personalisation Options. Saved content is private by default.

Location

With your consent, we collect GPS location to display your city on your profile, recommend nearby sellers, pre-fill delivery addresses, and show relevant live sessions. Your exact coordinates are never publicly shown — only city and state level. Revoke location permission through your device settings at any time.

Direct Messages

Messages are stored to enable conversation history. We do not routinely read private messages. We may review messages only where reported by a user, required by Nigerian law or court order, or where we suspect fraudulent or harmful activity. Our system may automatically detect and block messages containing external bank details to protect users from fraud.

Transactions & Wallet

We collect order details, payment amounts, delivery addresses, and all Wallet activity (deposits, withdrawals, transfers, escrow movements) for transaction processing, fraud detection, and CBN regulatory compliance. We do not store full bank card details.

Device & Security Information

We automatically collect device model, OS, IP address, network type, app version, session duration, and crash reports — used exclusively for security monitoring and improving Platform performance. Never used for advertising targeting.

Advertising

MistyNote does not run third-party advertising and does not share your data with external ad networks. Promoted listings in the Market are from Sellers who paid for featured placement and are clearly labelled as such.

IIHow We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to any third party. We share your data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Other Users: Public profile, posts, likes, and follows visible per your privacy settings.
  • Payment Providers: Necessary payment data shared with CBN-licensed processors (Paystack, Flutterwave, or similar) solely for payment processing.
  • Logistics Providers: Your delivery name, address, and phone shared only to complete delivery.
  • Identity Verification: Identity data shared with authorised verification services under strict confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose data to Nigerian law enforcement, CBN, NFIU, or courts where legally required or to prevent fraud or threats to safety.
  • Business Transfer: In a merger or acquisition, your data may transfer to the successor entity. We will notify you before any transfer takes effect.
IIIStorage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers in Nigeria or jurisdictions with equivalent data protection standards. We retain data while your account is active, plus additional periods required by Nigerian law.

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS) and at rest for sensitive data including passwords and identity documents.
  • Access controls limiting which employees can access personal data.
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments.
  • Automatic session timeouts and suspicious login detection.
  • Continuous Wallet monitoring with automatic freeze on suspicious activity.

If a security breach occurs that is likely to affect your rights, we will notify you and relevant Nigerian authorities as required by law.

IVManaging Your Information

Update most personal information directly through your profile and account settings. For a full export of your personal data, contact [email protected].

Privacy controls at Me → Settings → Privacy: who can see your posts and Moments; personalised content on/off; location shown on profile; notification preferences; read receipts in DMs; discoverability by contacts.

For automated decisions significantly affecting your rights (account suspension, seller tier demotion), you may request a human review at [email protected].

VAccount Closure & Data Deletion

Close your account at any time via Me → Settings → Account → Close Account. Before closing: withdraw any Wallet balance; ensure all orders are fulfilled or cancelled; resolve any pending disputes.

Upon closure, your public profile and posts are removed. Private data (messages, order history, Wallet records) is retained for the period required by Nigerian law — typically 5–7 years for financial records — then permanently deleted. Closed accounts cannot be recovered.

All providers sign data processing agreements limiting their use of your data to the specific service they provide.
